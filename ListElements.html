Angela's Recipe fot the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingridients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filing:

  • ¾ cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ½ talespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Leabe it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
  6. After the dough has doubled in size roll it out into a bowl.
